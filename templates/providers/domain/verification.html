To complete the setup of your custom domain, please verify that you own it by adding a DNS record.

Verification Instructions

Please add either a CNAME record or a TXT record to your domain's DNS settings:

Option 1: CNAME Record (Recommended)
Type Name/Host Target/Value TTL
CNAME book (or your subdomain) {{ request.get_host }} Auto
Option 2: TXT Record (Alternative)
Type Name/Host Value TTL
TXT @ (or your subdomain) {{ provider.domain_verification_code }} Auto

DNS changes may take up to 48 hours to propagate, though they're usually much faster.
